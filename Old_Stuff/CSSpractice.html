"Evening Serenity"

Softly falls the evening light,

Painting shadows, deep and bright,

Whispers of a day gone by,

Echo gently through the sky.

Stars appear, one by one,

Twinkling bright, like polished suns,

Moon rises, silver and serene,

Casting light on all unseen.

In this moment, time stands still,

All is calm, and all is still,

Peaceful, quiet, and serene,

A perfect end to a day, unseen.
